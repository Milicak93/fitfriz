FitFriz

Account deletion

This page explains how FitFriz users can request deletion of their account and what happens to associated data after deletion.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open the FitFriz app.
  2. Sign in to the account you want to delete.
  3. Open Settings.
  4. Select the delete-account option.
  5. Confirm the deletion request.
Account deletion is permanent. After the request is completed, the action cannot be undone.

What data is deleted

When account deletion succeeds, FitFriz deletes the user account record and associated data stored through the current application data model, including:

  • account profile data;
  • saved preferences;
  • saved pantry items;
  • authentication sessions and related account-action tokens; and
  • weekly planning records linked to the deleted account.

What may remain for a limited time

Some limited technical records may remain temporarily in backups, logs, or third-party operational systems where retention is required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or disaster recovery.

Delete local app data

If you also want to remove information stored on your device, delete your account in the app, then uninstall the app or clear app storage from Android system settings.
